What data can be deleted

NexuOne stores data primarily on the user’s device, such as:

  • Saved FTP connections (host, port, username and password if saved)
  • Scanned images and generated PDF documents
  • Notes / To-do content

We do not store your documents or FTP credentials on our servers.

How to delete your data

  1. Open the app and delete the content you want to remove (e.g., documents/notes).
  2. To remove FTP data: open the FTP section and delete saved connections.
  3. To remove all local app data: uninstall NexuOne from your device.

Retention

Local data is kept on the device until the user deletes it or uninstalls the app. We do not retain user content on our servers.
